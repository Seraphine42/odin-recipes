Description

This is a great comfort food for when you need a quick pick me up!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz package of hot dogs
  • 28 oz can of baked beans (any flavor)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Slice the hotdogs into small .5-.75 inch pieces.
  2. Cook in a pan over medium-high heat until browned, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add baked beans, brown sugar, yellow mustard, and garlic powder. Mix well.
  4. Serve!
